Who are ILLIT?

ILLIT is a South Korean girl group formed under Belift Lab (a subsidiary of HYBE Labels). The group officially debuted on March 25, 2024 with their first mini album SUPER REAL ME. ILLIT is composed of five members — Yunah, Minju, Moka, Wonhee, and Iroha — who were selected through the survival show R U Next?. Known for their fresh and youthful concept, the group quickly gained attention for their powerful performances, unique style, and the support of HYBE’s global platform.
